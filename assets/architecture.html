IV. Ensemble Meta-Learner · Cross-Domain Integration
Ensemble
Meta-Model Component 4 · Vision
Combines outputs from technical, fundamental, and sentiment models into a unified forward-return prediction per ticker. Regime-aware weighting — different domains dominate in different market conditions. Calibrated probability output suitable for portfolio construction.

Training: walk-forward validation. Feature engineering: per-domain scores + regime features (VIX, sector dispersion, market trend). Frameworks: stacking with scikit-learn or gradient-boosted meta-learners.
